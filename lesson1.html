Why is Your Computer Running Slowly?

A slow computer could mean one of several things. If your computer has not been rebooted recently it could just need to reboot. You may have backup programs, such a startup programs and anti-virus scanners running; disable all unnecessary ones. You may have too many programs installed or running. Many computers have temporary files that are not being used for anything. Manually deleting these files could speed your computer. Your hard drive could be damaged or filled. Your computer may be infected with viruses or malware. If you have Windows, your computer may need to be updated. You may need to upgrade your computerâ€™s Random Access Memory.
