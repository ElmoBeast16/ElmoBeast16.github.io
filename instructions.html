How to Play:
To play, all you need to do is use the WASD keys for single player and use arrow keys for the second player to play. Using the keys, the goal of the game is to hit the ball into the opponents net. At certain intervals there will be power-ups that will fall from the sky and they will give you better abilities that will assist you in the ability to win the game.
